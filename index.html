PZ Park in Wolfeboro (Coming in 2026)

Native gardens and trails bringing people and nature together.

Mission

Imagine a weed-choked lot in Wolfeboro reborn as a thriving, beautifully landscaped public park—a sanctuary of vibrant flowers, lush shrubs, majestic trees, and winding paths that invite connection, reflection, and discovery. PZ Park's mission is to create this enduring oasis, centered around a cherished gazebo, where bees, birds, and butterflies flourish alongside our community.

By investing in PZ Park, you're not just funding a park—you're seeding a legacy of environmental stewardship, native habitat restoration, and community unity. This one-acre haven will offer a space for recreation, light education, and volunteer engagement, cultivating a deep appreciation for nature's vital role in a healthy, thriving Wolfeboro. Your support will transform a forgotten corner into a vibrant, pollinator-friendly park that inspires joy, connection, and contentment for generations to come. Join us in bringing PZ Park to life—contact us today to learn more.
