Mission

PZ Park aims to establish a beautifully landscaped public park in downtown Wolfeboro, featuring pollinator-friendly, lush plantings of shrubs, bushes, evergreens, trees, and vibrant flowers connected by inviting walking paths. The park is dedicated to fostering community connection, environmental conservation, and the restoration of native habitats while providing open spaces for public enjoyment, volunteer stewardship, and light educational opportunities. PZ Park's commitment to attracting bees, birds, and butterflies cultivates a lasting appreciation for nature and the vital role it plays in a healthy and thriving community.

Purpose

The primary purpose of PZ Park is to create, maintain, and operate a publicly accessible landscaped park in Wolfeboro, New Hampshire, featuring a diverse array of plant life and walking paths. This will provide the local community with a space for recreation, relaxation, and environmental education while promoting the conservation of native habitats. PZ Park encourages community involvement through volunteer opportunities and seeks to be a catalyst for fostering a deeper connection between the people of Wolfeboro and the natural world.
